Capsule Defender

About this game:

This is another Friday Night Game Jam entry that I made in 3 hours. It was one of my first attempts at a top down wave survival game. It's incredibly scuffed, buggy but it's still somewhat finished. There exists one type of enemy, basic movement, one room, an incredibly scuffed health system and a basic time survived counter. It's definitely one my more simplier games but I definitely enjoyed making this game.

You can play the game at my itch.io page: bigmemerman1.itch.io
